My Time at Maxidata

February 28, 2025

It was a weird time‚Äîeverything felt new, and on top of that, the world was in the middle of a global pandemic.

What I Built#

Over the years, I got to work on some really cool projects.

Building this wasn‚Äôt just about working on the UI. A lot of backend work had to happen too.
