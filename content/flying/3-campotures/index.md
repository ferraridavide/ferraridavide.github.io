Just got back from 3 days in Campo Tures, flying from Ahornach. First day was nothing special, but day two was great! Got 50 minutes in the air, topped out around 2,200m. Super happy with that!

{{< igc-viewer track="track.igc" id="flight-1" >}}

I did make one pretty dumb mistake though. I’d been waiting at launch for hours in shorts and t-shirt, no gloves. It was already 5pm, sun starting to drop, and I was harnessed up ready to go. My friends were just staring at me like… bro, you’re really gonna fly like that?

I went anyway thinking it was just gonna be a short flight like the others. Big mistake. 2,200m in a t-shirt is no joke, I ended up bailing out earlier than I wanted to because I was freezing.
